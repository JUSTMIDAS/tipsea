Our Story

A private sanctuary
unlike any other

Nestled at the southern tip of Elbow Cay, TipSea Point has been a treasured hideaway for those who seek something beyond the ordinary. The property commands unobstructed views across the Sea of Abaco and the Atlantic — two distinct bodies of water meeting at your doorstep.

With access to pristine private beaches, world-class bonefishing flats, and the charming pastel streets of Hope Town just minutes away, this estate offers a rare synthesis of total seclusion and curated adventure.

Frequently Asked

Questions &
Answers

How do we get to TipSea Point?+
Fly into Marsh Harbour International (MHH) from Miami, Nassau, or Fort Lauderdale. From Marsh Harbour, take Albury's Ferry to Hope Town (~15 min) — or we can arrange a private boat transfer. A golf cart will meet you at the dock and deliver you directly to the estate.
Is the entire property private?+
Yes. TipSea Point is rented exclusively — no shared facilities, no other guests. The entire estate, beach, dock, and grounds are yours for the duration of your stay.
Can we arrange a private chef?+
Absolutely. We work with several exceptional local chefs who can be booked to cook daily, prepare a welcome dinner, or handle specific meals during your stay. Just let us know in your booking request and we'll make the arrangements.
What water activities are available?+
From the property you have access to kayaks and snorkeling equipment. We can arrange bone fishing and deep-sea charters, boat rentals, spearfishing excursions, diving with Brendal's Dive Center, and day trips to uninhabited cays in the area.
What is your cancellation policy?+
Cancellations made 60+ days before arrival receive a full refund minus a $200 processing fee. Cancellations 30–60 days prior forfeit 50% of the total. Within 30 days, no refund is issued. We strongly recommend travel insurance for international bookings.
Is the property suitable for children?+
TipSea Point is a wonderful family destination. Children enjoy the calm-water access, the putting green, and the shallow bay area. We ask that children be supervised near the dock and deeper water areas. Please mention children's ages when booking so we can prepare appropriately.
